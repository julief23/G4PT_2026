About the G4PT Model

What is G4PT? Qu’est-ce que G4PT ? Che cos’è G4PT? ¿Qué es G4PT?

G4PT (G-Quadruplex Prediction and Generation Tool) is an AI-powered molecular design platform that combines large language models (LLMs), generative chemistry, and machine learning to accelerate the discovery of G-quadruplex (G4)-stabilizing compounds. G4s, prevalent in telomeres and oncogene promoters, are promising therapeutic targets, but the identification of selective ligands remains a major challenge in medicinal chemistry.

Built upon the AGAPE framework and a curated dataset of experimentally characterized compounds, G4PT enables users to interact with the platform using natural language prompts, molecule names, or SMILES representations. The system automatically extracts molecular constraints, identifies reference molecules through cheminformatics databases, and leverages a fine-tuned MolGPT generative model to design novel candidate compounds that satisfy user-defined requirements.

Generated molecules undergo multiple validation and filtering stages, including RDKit structural verification, physicochemical property assessment, and reliability checks. The validated compounds are subsequently evaluated by AGAPE, which predicts their probability of stabilizing G4 structures using machine learning models trained on experimentally validated G4 ligands.

Candidate molecules are ranked according to their predicted G4-stabilization potential and relevant molecular properties before being presented to the user. An iterative feedback loop allows researchers to refine their design objectives and generate improved compounds through successive rounds of optimization.

By integrating natural language interaction, molecular generation, cheminformatics validation, and predictive modeling into a unified workflow, G4PT provides a robust and interpretable platform for AI-assisted discovery of novel G4-targeting molecules and highlights the value of combining chemical expertise with modern generative AI approaches.
