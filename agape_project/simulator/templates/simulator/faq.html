AGAPE is a machine-learning-based predictive platform designed to estimate ligand–G-quadruplex affinity using classical and quantum chemical descriptors. It integrates curated molecular features with trained predictive models.

AGAPE supports:
  • CSV upload of classical descriptors (AGAPE v1.0)
  • SMILES input with integrated molecule editor (AGAPE v2.0)

Lightweight Model (180 features) is faster and recommended for quick evaluation.

Comprehensive Model (489 descriptors) uses the full feature set and may provide improved predictive resolution at the cost of computation time.

In AGAPE v2.0, molecular structures are drawn or pasted as SMILES. Classical descriptors are automatically computed from the molecular structure before being passed to the predictive model.

Uploaded descriptor files are processed for prediction purposes but not stored. Please refer to the Privacy Policy for details on data handling and retention.
