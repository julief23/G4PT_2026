3. Purpose of the Platform

AGAPE is a computational platform designed to predict the potential of small molecules to bind and stabilize G-quadruplex (G4) DNA structures using machine learning and deep learning models.

The results provided by the platform are intended for research purposes only and do not replace experimental validation.

5. Personal Data Protection

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we are committed to protecting personal data processed through this website.

AGAPE is designed to minimize the collection of personal data and to process only the information necessary for the proper functioning of the platform.

5.1. Data Collected

The platform may process the following data:

  • Molecular input data submitted by users, such as SMILES strings or uploaded CSV files
  • Technical session data necessary for form submission and result delivery
  • Basic server-side logs required for security, maintenance, and troubleshooting
5.2. Purpose of Processing

Data is processed exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Running molecular predictions requested by users
  • Generating and displaying prediction results
  • Ensuring the technical functioning, security, and reliability of the platform
  • Improving the quality and maintenance of the service
5.3. Data Retention Period

Submitted data and generated results may be stored temporarily for the time necessary to process the user request and deliver the output.

Session-related data may be deleted automatically when the browser session ends or after a limited technical retention period.

5.4. Legal Basis for Processing

The processing of data on this platform is based on:

  • User consent when molecular data is submitted for prediction
  • Legitimate interest in operating, securing, and improving the platform
  • Technical necessity for processing user requests
