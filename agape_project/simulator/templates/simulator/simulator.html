For complex molecules, submitting a SMILES string directly through the JSME pasting option may produce different prediction results compared to single SMILES input.
The JSME editor automatically reinterprets and reformats SMILES strings. Although the chemical structure remains equivalent, the textual representation may differ slightly.
Since molecular descriptors are computed from the exact SMILES representation, even small formatting differences can lead to variations in calculated descriptors and therefore affect the final prediction.
